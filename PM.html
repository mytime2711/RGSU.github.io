Samdech Thipadei Hun Manet
Prime Minister of Cambodia

Hun Manet (Khmer: ហ៊ុន ម៉ាណែត ; born 20 October 1977) is a Cambodian politician and general who has served as the prime minister of Cambodia since August 2023, succeeding his father, Hun Sen.

H.E. Dr. Hun Manet is a Cambodian military official who joined the Royal Cambodian Armed Forces (RCAF) in 1995 and later studied at the U.S. Military Academy in West Point. In 1999, he was appointed as Deputy Chief of Bodyguard of the Prime Minister of Cambodia. In 2008, H.E. Dr. Hun Manet was appointed as a Colonel in the RCAF and became Commander of the Anti-Terrorism Special Forces. From 2008 to 2011, he played a vital role in negotiations and combat tactics as the Cambodian army faced an armed confrontation with the Thai army at the Preah Vihear Temple. He also encouraged young people to join the army and promoted equality among army members. H.E. Dr. Hun Manet was promoted to Lieutenant General in 2013 and appointed as Deputy Commander of the Royal Cambodian Army, RCAF. In 2018, he was appointed as Joint Deputy Chief of Staff and Acting Commander of the Royal Cambodian Army, and later as Deputy Commander-in-Chief and Commander of the Royal Cambodian Army, in addition to his existing duties. H.E. Dr. Hun Manet's contributions to the Cambodian army have been significant, from his early days as Deputy Chief of Bodyguard to his current positions as Deputy Commander-in-Chief and Commander of the Royal Cambodian Army. He has been instrumental in negotiating during times of conflict, implementing reforms, and improving the army's efficiency and inclusivity.

H.E. Dr. Hun Manet's Family

H.E. Dr. Hun Manet, Lork Chumteav Dr. Pich Chanmony, and their children

H.E. Dr. Hun Manet was born on October 20, 1977, in Samrong Tbong Village, Treak Commune, Memot District, Kampong Cham Province (currently known as Tbong Khmum Province). He completed his primary education at Komarey Primary and Secondary School (formerly known as Chaktomuk Primary and Secondary School) between 1980 and 1990. H.E. Dr. Hun Manet then pursued his secondary education at Phnom Daun Penh High School (formerly known as Preah Sisowath High School) from 1990 to 1993. After completing his high school education, H.E. Dr. Hun Manet enrolled in the United States Military Academy in West Point, where he successfully graduated on May 29, 1999, becoming the first Cambodian to do so. He subsequently earned a Master of Arts in Economics from the University of New York in 2002, and later completed his Ph.D. in Economics at the University of Bristol in the United Kingdom in 2008. In terms of his personal life, H.E. Dr. Hun Manet married Lork Chumteav Dr. Pich Chanmony on January 7, 2006, and is a proud father to three children: Hun Pich Monyvorleak (born on July 25, 2007), Hun Pich Monylyna (born on September 1, 2010), and Hun Pich Monyrithiseth (born on October 9, 2017).
